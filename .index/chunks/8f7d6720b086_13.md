 *   - The permutations for the third row are `[[A,B1,C1], [A,B1,C2], [A,B1,C3], [A,B2,C1], [A,B2,C2], [A,B2,C3]]`
 *
 * @param listOfLists - The list of lists of elements that represent the allOf, anyOf or oneOf resolved values in order
